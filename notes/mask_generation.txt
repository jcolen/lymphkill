lymphkill/mask_generation
-Uses contours generated from structure_loading and CT, Dose files to build the mask_generation

get_first_CT_frame(ct_infos)
    get first z-oriented slice from CT file

get_conversion_grids(ct_info, dose_info, dim_vol, dim_dos)
    do some fancy math to  onvert between CT and Dose dimensions

find_matching_contour_idx(contours, name_strings)
    finds the index for a given organ in the contours structure by searching name_strings

layer_size(mask)
    calculate the size of the layer at the passed in mask
